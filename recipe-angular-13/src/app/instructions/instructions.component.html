Black Bean Fajitas

| Serves 4 | Prep 5 minutes | Cook 6 hours |

Quantity Ingredient
{{ item.quantity }} {{ item.ingredient }}

Freezer Meal Preparation

  1. Chop onion to desired size
  2. Drain and rinse black beans
  3. Add Black beans and onion to large freezer safe bag
  4. Mix spices together with the black beans and onion
  5. Flatten and store in the freezer

Cooking Directions

  1. Defrost in fridge over night
  2. Place in slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hours
  3. Serve on torillas and top with Guac, Sour Cream, and Cheese
